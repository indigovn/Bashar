You don't change the world — you change yourself and shift to a parallel Earth that already matches your new vibration. Memory is consciousness time travel into the past, psychic ability is consciousness time travel into the future, and your passion is the rudder that navigates you through the infinite frames to the path of least resistance.

## Unified Key Principles

### On the Now
- There is only one moment — now. There is only one place — here.
- Space reduces to "here." Time reduces to "now." Everything else is different frequencies of here and now.
- Everything exists here and now — all information, all lives, all realities.
