# Bashar: Unconditional Love — Everything Is Created from Love - Complete Teaching

## Overview

This document is a comprehensive compilation of Bashar's (and related channelers') complete teachings on love, drawn from across all documented transmissions. It reveals love not as an emotion but as the **vibratory frequency of existence itself** — the fundamental energy from which all of creation emerges. Every heartbeat transmits it, every being is made of it, and the universe itself is its expression. The teachings progress from what love actually is at the universal level, through how it operates as the heart's electromagnetic field, how it connects to the higher mind and passion, how it manifests as self-worth and tears and prayer, how it relates to fear as its distortion, and ultimately how dissolving into love opens the portal to the soul. The core message: you don't *find* love — you ARE love. You don't *earn* love — your existence IS creation's love expressed.
