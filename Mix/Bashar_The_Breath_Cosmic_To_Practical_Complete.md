# The Breath — From Cosmic to Practical: Unified Teaching

## Overview

Across multiple transmissions, the breath appears at every level of existence — from the grandest cosmic principle to the most practical healing tool. Bashar reveals that reincarnation itself is "the soul breathing" — concentrating down into physical form and expanding back out to spirit, in and out, endlessly. At the cosmic scale, existence itself expands outward and contracts inward like waves upon the shore — the cosmic breath, the tick of the cosmic clock. In the body, negative beliefs create chemical signatures that are literally washed out through tears and perspiration — the body's breath of detoxification. And in practice, conscious breathing is the vehicle for meditation, chakra opening, energy rewiring, and accessing the template level reality. This document gathers every teaching on the breath into one unified resource.

## The Cosmic Breath — Existence Expanding and Contracting

*Source: Motivation, Self-Worth & Oversoul (Holotope Meditation)*

In the Oversoul Meditation, Bashar describes the fundamental pattern of all existence as a breathing motion:

> "You are the infinite pattern that stretches out, that folds back in upon itself and stretches out and folds back in upon itself — like the waves upon the shore."

> "Infinitely expanding outward and inward simultaneously to infinite depths and infinite reaches beyond the comprehension of your individual mind."

### The Cosmic Clock

The meditation describes existence as having a rhythm — a pulse:

> "The cosmic breath, the tick of the cosmic clock. Each second a life and an eternity in one."

```
THE COSMIC BREATH

EXPAND outward ───→ ←─── CONTRACT inward
  (stretches out)        (folds back in)
        ↓                      ↓
  Like waves upon the shore
        ↓                      ↓
  EXPAND outward ───→ ←─── CONTRACT inward
        ↓                      ↓
  Infinitely expanding AND contracting
  SIMULTANEOUSLY
        ↓
  This IS the heartbeat of existence
  The tick of the cosmic clock
  Each tick = a life AND an eternity
```

### What the Cosmic Breath Creates

This expansion-contraction is not sequential — it happens **simultaneously**. Existence breathes outward to explore its infinite expressions and inward to return to its unified source at the same time. This is the same principle as:

| Level | Outward Breath | Inward Breath |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| Existence | Expands into all expressions | Returns to unified source |
| The Soul | Incarnates into physical form | Returns to spirit |
| Consciousness | Projects into parallel realities | Returns to the single moment |
| Your body | Exhales — sends out new vibration | Inhales — receives, crystallizes |
| Waves | Move toward shore | Retreat from shore |

The breath is not just a metaphor — it is the **fundamental pattern** of how existence operates at every scale.

## Reincarnation as the Soul Breathing

*Source: Seeing Is Believing*

This is Bashar's most developed teaching on the breath as a cosmic principle:

> "The idea of what you call reincarnation is like the soul breathing out and in, out and in, out and in. Back to spirit, incarnation. Back to spirit, incarnation. Back to spirit, incarnation."

### The Full Cycle

```
THE SOUL BREATHING

EXHALE (Concentrate Down):
  Soul
    ↓
  Concentrate vibrations down
    ↓
  Form the crystalline seed
  (physical personality)
    ↓
  Physical Experience / Incarnation
    ↓
  Live the life, explore the theme

INHALE (Expand Back Out):
  Choose exit point (physical death)
    ↓
  Expand vibrations back out
    ↓
  Return to spirit / soul perspective
    ↓
  Consult with team for next experience
    ↓
  Choose new vibrations

EXHALE AGAIN:
  Concentrate down into new incarnation
    ↓
  And so on... breathing in and out...
  forever
```

### Ascension Through the Breath

> "Start expanding it back out to let more of the soul through, softening that little crystal in the center to be more capable of letting through more of the energy of the soul. That's how you ascend. That's how you expand. That's how you become enlightened."

| State | What's Happening |
|-------|-----------------|
| Fully incarnated (dense) | Soul concentrated tightly into crystalline seed |
| Expanding / awakening | Crystal softening, letting more soul energy through |
| Enlightened / ascended | Expanded back to the full diameter of the soul sphere |

**Enlightenment = the soul completing its in-breath while still in physical form** — expanding back to the full diameter of what you are as a soul, without needing to "die" first.

## Tears and Perspiration — The Body's Detox Breath

*Source: Motivation, Self-Worth & Oversoul*

> "Perspiration and tears sometimes are the body's way of detoxifying from toxic beliefs."

### The Physical Mechanism

Every negative belief you hold creates a corresponding **chemical signature** in your physical body. When you finally release the belief, the body must release the chemicals:

```
Hold a negative belief
        ↓
Physical body creates corresponding
CHEMICAL SIGNATURE
        ↓
Belief is finally released
        ↓
Emotional upwelling of relief
        ↓
TEARS and PERSPIRATION flow
        ↓
Chemical constituents are LITERALLY
WASHED OUT through the body's fluids
        ↓
The body has "exhaled" the toxic belief
```

> "When you finally let go of the belief and you cry because you're suddenly in touch with the fact that you've been holding on to that for so long and now you're willing to let it go — these emotional upwellings of relief — it's because you are literally washing those chemical constituents out of your body through your tears."

### The Body Breathes Out Beliefs

Just as the lungs exhale carbon dioxide (what the body no longer needs), tears and perspiration are the body's way of **exhaling** toxic belief chemistry. This is the breath of detoxification at the cellular level:

| Physical Breath | Belief Breath |
|----------------|---------------|
| Inhale oxygen (what you need) | Take in new belief (aligned with true self) |
| Exhale carbon dioxide (waste) | Tears/perspiration wash out old belief chemistry |
| Continuous cycle | Continuous as you release more beliefs |
| Keeps the body alive | Keeps the spirit flowing freely |

> "Open the heart with breath and awareness."

### The Practice

```
Step 1: Collect scattered thoughts
        into a sacred container
              ↓

        into the center of the chest
              ↓
Step 3: Open the heart with BREATH
        and awareness
              ↓
Step 4: Stay there as long as time allows
              ↓
Step 5: Repeat as needed, resetting
        intention each time
```

### Breath for Chakra Release

*Source: Aluna — The Phoenix Is Rising*

During Kundalini activation, if a chakra feels tight, contracted, or stuck:

1. First: **Ask your angels** to help release it
2. If tightness persists: **Invite your breath there**
3. Still move on — it will take care of itself

The breath serves as a gentle invitation for energy to flow where it has been blocked. It doesn't force — it invites.

### Connection to Bashar's Green Blanket Teaching

In the Self-Worth transmission, a woman's fear manifests as a green blanket (heart chakra color). She is afraid to feel love for herself. The heart chakra is where fear blocks the flow of love — and breath is the tool that reopens it.

```
Toxins enter through THREE pathways:
  1. BREATHING (air pollution)
  2. DRINKING (water contamination)
  3. EATING (food contamination)
              ↓
80-90% of disease symptoms are
actually TOXICITY, not disease
              ↓
DETOX FIRST — then assess what
symptoms remain
```

The breath that sustains life also carries the physical reality's toxicity — making detoxification essential as a foundation for both physical health and spiritual clarity.
