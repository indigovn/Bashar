## Context: Why the Soul, Why Now

### A First for Eluña

This transmission marks the first time Eluña has publicly channeled directly from her own soul, rather than from extraterrestrial beings, councils, or interdimensional entities. She describes it as "a little bit nerve-wracking":

> "While I have a lot of practice channeling publicly with extraterrestrial beings and different councils and also with interdimensional beings such as elementals, I have never sat down to just express from my soul necessarily in this way."

### The Realization

During her morning meditation in January 2026, contemplating recent world events and the collective unease they have caused, Eluña came to a realization:

> "The only real connection that we need is to our soul."
