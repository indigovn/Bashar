# The Three Guides (via Aluna): The Kybalion — The Principle of Correspondence - Complete Teaching

## Overview

This is the second transmission in the "Mysteries of the Kybalion" series, channeled through Aluna from her three spirit guides — initiates and teachers of Hermetic wisdom. It explores the Kybalion's second principle: **"As Above, So Below; As Below, So Above"** — the Principle of Correspondence. The guides examine this law through the lens of the microcosm (your cells, your body, your mind) reflecting the macrocosm (the cosmos, higher planes). They deliver a striking teaching on cellular consciousness, sovereignty over one's own body, the evolution of the self through the metaphor of seed-to-fruit, and the conditions required to receive higher truth. The core message: evolution begins within, and the state of your inner world determines what the higher planes can reach you with.

## The Consciousness of Your Cells

### What Your Cells Are

The guides present cells as conscious beings with their own awareness and purpose:

> "Each one of your cells hums, vibrates, dances in its own unique way — performing various functions through your body and in your being, completely unaware that you are to these functions."

| What Cells Do | What They Don't Need |
|---|---|
| Hum, vibrate, dance uniquely | Your conscious thought |
| Perform functions autonomously | Your mental energy |
| Know their part in the cosmic dance | Your studying or mental aptitude |
| Help the body, move parts, excrete waste | Your commands (at the basic level) |
| Keep you alive | Your understanding |
| Allow you to experience the Earth plane | Your awareness of them |

> "They know their part to play in the cosmic dance, in the cosmic order of things. They carry on with their work — with their life's work."

### The Mystery

> "And yet to most they are mysterious. They are not commonly studied in your schools. In fact, they are very much misunderstood."

> "Cells are able to perform and behave in a multitude of ways that is not yet understood by most humans."

### Level 1: Learning the Language of the Cells

> "They do respond to your commands — if only one knows how to speak their language."

- Requires a basic understanding of how cells work

## The Seed, the Sprout, the Flower, the Fruit

### The Evolution Metaphor

The guides address the parts of the self that resist change — the ego selves — through a profound nature metaphor:

> "The different selves that are in you — they want to rest. They want to stay in the same state. They do not wish to change. They do not wish to evolve. Evolution is understood by them as the dying of them."

### Yet Nature Shows the Way

```
  SEED
    ↓  (the seed does not mourn itself)
  SPROUT
    ↓  (the sprout does not mourn the seed)
  FLOWER
    ↓  (the flower does not mourn the sprout)
  FRUIT
    ↓  (no mourning the loss of the petals)
  NEW LIFE
    ↓  (and what emerges from there
        is even more life)
```

> "The seed, the sprout, and even the flower — they do not fear the evolution. They do not wish to be anything but what they are."

> "They know there is far more beyond than what they have experienced previously."

> "And even when the beauty of the flower drops, and in its place there is a fruit — there is no mourning the loss of the petals. There is only rejoicing. A celebration of new life."

### The Lesson for the Self

> "This is the opportunity that is available to all humanity."

The ego-selves fear evolution because they perceive it as death. But nature demonstrates that each stage gives way to something **more** — and each transition is cause for rejoicing, not mourning.

## The Conditions for Purity of Spirit

## Key Principles Summary

### On the Principle of Correspondence
- As Above, So Below; As Below, So Above
- Every microscopic evolution affects the cosmic whole
- Understanding the microcosm (your cells, body, mind) reveals the macrocosm
- The boundaries between planes have blurred as human consciousness has grown

### On Cellular Consciousness
- Your cells are conscious beings performing their cosmic dance autonomously
- They respond to your commands if you learn to speak their language
- At higher attunement, you access the universal language — think, and cells respond
- Understanding cellular function is the **next stage of human evolution**

### On Sovereignty
- Relying on outside sources for body knowledge is "one of the lowest forms of allowing your free will to be taken"
- True sovereignty requires understanding the health, status, and state of your own being
- Ask yourself: How are you eating? Drinking? Sleeping?
- The body must be in a state to receive higher transmissions

### On Evolution and the Self
- The ego-selves resist change because they perceive evolution as death
- Nature shows: seed → sprout → flower → fruit — each stage gives way to more life, with no mourning
- Evolution of the entire being happens through the mind
- If the body or ego controls the mind, evolution is blocked or slowed

### On Receiving Truth
- Truth comes in whispers and small sips — not all at once
- Stay continuously open; do not cling to truth as understood in any single moment
- Teachers shift throughout your life — allow the twists and turns
- All paths lead to the same ocean, if the seeker remains open

### On Purity
- Purity is not killing off parts of yourself — it is **awareness** of all parts
- Requires softness **and** a strong will — both
- No egoic attachments that you are **unaware** of
- Always be vigilant of your mental, emotional, and physical state
