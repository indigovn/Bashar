A questioner shares that his friend woke up terrified hearing him speak in his sleep — what sounded like five different conversations simultaneously, in an unknown language, all sounding angry.

> "You may be working certain processes and experiences out in your dream state that thus then makes it so you don't have to experience the manifestation of those particular things in your physical reality."

**4. Out-of-Body Conversations**

> "You may be making many different cross connections to many different dimensions of realities or what you would call parallel reality lives, what you typically call past lives, future lives. You may be extracting information from them and processing them through yourself to find the bits that are relevant for this life here."

```
SLEEP STATE
    ├── Processing beliefs & experiences

    ├── Substitute experiences (so you don't need them physically)
```

> "It is almost always a combination of things. What portion of it, what version of it, what level of it someone else may experience will be the level that's relevant for them. But that doesn't mean you're not doing other things as well that are relevant for you."

## The Spring Room Analogy — The Physics of Resistance

### The Analogy

> "It's like being in a small room and the walls are covered with giant springs. And if you try to push away what you say you don't prefer, you only build up energy in those springs and they will snap back a hundredfold."

### The Solution

> "But if you simply let them be the springs they are and say 'nice spring, nice spring, you are valid as you are' — thus then by allowing all the springs to be equal, you can experience which one you prefer in a peaceful, joyful, creative state because you're not in a sense forcing your attention on getting rid of something."

```
RESISTANCE APPROACH:
Push spring → Energy builds → SNAPS BACK 100x
        ↓
More of what you don't want

ALLOWANCE APPROACH:
"Nice spring, you are valid" → All springs equal → Freedom of choice
        ↓
Experience what you prefer peacefully
```

### The Principle of Full Allowance

> "You are allowing it in and thus then by full allowance you don't actually have to experience that thing because now you have freedom of choice."

> "There is nowhere to push anything away to."

### Automatic Manifestation Through Being

> "As long as that choice is truly representative of what you truly believe to be most reflective of who you are, then that will simply be what automatically happens. You don't even have to think about it. You don't have to wonder about it. You don't even have to in a sense make it happen or arrange things to happen that way. You just need to keep being yourself."

### What Won't Go Away Until You Get the Point

> "Anytime you walk away, push away from what is as if somehow it didn't belong, that it wasn't right, it wasn't valid, it will always stick around until you get the point."

> "That's how the engine works. That's how the mechanism works. And it always works that way. Never fails."
