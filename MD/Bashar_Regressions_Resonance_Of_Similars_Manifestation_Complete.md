## Regressions and Higher Self Communication

### Context: Dolores Cannon's QHHT Method

A practitioner of Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique (QHHT) describes the process:
1. Client is guided through "regressions" — experiencing past, present, or future lives (all simultaneous)
2. Client may experience being people, rocks, insects, or simply energy
3. A deep state is reached where the "higher self" speaks through the client in third person
4. The higher self answers pre-prepared questions about emotional, spiritual, and physical issues
5. The higher self ties answers back to the regression experiences

### The Higher Mind as Go-Between

> "Even if there are other beings involved — even like myself with the channel — I still have to work with the being's own higher mind to deliver the information. The higher mind of the being has to be the go-between to deliver the information."

```
Other Beings / Entities / Aspects
              ↓
    Higher Mind (Go-Between)
              ↓
    Being must be WILLING
              ↓
    Information Delivered
              ↓
    Conscious Awareness
```

> "It's the information that is necessary, not necessarily its source."

### Bleed-Through from Concurrent Lives

The regression process involves energetic and informational connections that a person has made to other entities across simultaneous lives. The information and energy "bleeds through."

### Any Change Is a Total Change

### Guidance for Facilitators

**The key**: Let their own higher minds tell you what is needed.

## Manifestation Across Realities — The Tesseract Analogy

### The Core Problem

A questioner wants to bring the energy of experiences from other realities (regressions, dreams, altered states — eagles, unicorns, etc.) into this physical reality in the same form.

### Bashar's Key Correction

> "You can manifest the energy of that reality in the form in which it will work where you are. It doesn't have to look like it does on the other level. It needs to look like it needs to look on the level that you're operating on, but the energy will be the same."

> "You're just under the mistaken impression that it has to look the same. Doesn't look the same on different levels, but it's the same energy."

### The Tesseract Illustration

```
YOUR REALITY (3D):          HIGHER REALITY (4D+):
┌─────────────┐              ┌───┬───┬───┐
│             │              │ C │ C │ C │
│  One Cube   │              │ u │ u │ u │
│  (transparent│     ←→      │ b │ b │ b │
│   all sides) │              │ e │ e │ e │
│             │              │ 1 │ 2 │ 3 │...
└─────────────┘              └───┴───┴───┘
                              (Multiple cubes
                               overlapping in
                               same space)
```

- In your reality: a transparent cube is just one cube from every direction
- On a higher level: each face of the cube leads to a **different cube** — a tesseract
- A tesseract doesn't manifest in your reality the same way
- But **the same energy** can give you **the same effect** through the symbol that works in your reality

### The Unicorn Principle

> "What can easily look like a unicorn on one level might only show up in your reality as a horse. But if it's the same energy, if it's the symbol that represents the same state of being, why ignore the fact that the energy has come to you?"

| Higher Reality Symbol | This Reality Symbol | Same Energy? |
|----------------------|---------------------|-------------|
| Unicorn | Horse | Yes |
| Tesseract | Cube | Yes |
| Eagle/mythical form | What works here | Yes |

> "You've manifested it, but you've manifested it in the symbol that works in your reality so that it will work in your reality."

### The Practical Method

```
Step 1: Remember the FEELING of the experience
        (not the visual form)
              ↓
Step 2: Enter that STATE OF BEING
              ↓
Step 3: ACT from that state of being
              ↓
Step 4: The universe manifests whatever FORM
        is appropriate for this reality
              ↓
Result: Same energy, same effect — different appearance
```

> "All you have to do is remember what that felt like and you'll be in the same state of being. Then acting from that state of being, you will manifest whatever form it needs to look like in this reality."

### Critical Insight

> "Manifestation also has to do with the type of reality or dimension. Absolutely."

## Fear vs. Intuition — Stop Smashing Them Together

### The Problem

People feel they can't tell the difference between fear and intuition because the two seem like the same voice.

### Bashar's Reframe

> "Don't they feel different? Or is it that you have an intuition and then you have a fear about it? That's different."

### The Key Distinction

```
WRONG framework:
  Fear and Intuition = ONE blurred experience
  (Can't tell them apart)

RIGHT framework:
  1. Intuition arrives (clear knowing)
  2. Fear arises SEPARATELY as a reaction to the intuition
  (Two distinct experiences — easy to work with)
```

> "If you don't assume that it's difficult to tell the difference between fear and intuition, but that you know when you're having an intuition, and then you can recognize when you're having fear about what you're intuiting, that may give you something easier to work with."

### The Definition Creates the Experience

> "Your definition is smashing them together making it more difficult to tell them apart. So have a definition where you say, 'No, no, no — my intuition is clear when it's happening, but I realize that separately I'm having a fear reaction to it.' Well, now I have something to work with because I can separate the two and see them both clearly for what they are."

| Old Definition | New Definition |
|---------------|---------------|
| "I can't tell fear from intuition" | "My intuition is clear; fear is a separate reaction" |
| One blurred experience | Two distinct experiences |
| Paralysis — can't act | Clarity — can investigate the fear |
| Focus on confusion | Focus on the negative belief generating the fear |

## Learning the Right Way — When Forgetting Is a Message

### The Presenting Problem

A nutrition consultant building a business experiences:
- Complete blanks on words she knows well (e.g., "celiac," "gluten")
- Loss of confidence during client sessions
- Feeling stunned and incompetent in moments that should be routine
- History of head and spinal injuries raising concern about physical causes

### The Diagnosis

> "You're studying incorrectly for you and that's why you're not knowing what you need to know when you need to know it."

> "When you force yourself to learn things in a way that doesn't work for you, one of the side effects is forgetting what you already know."

### The Mechanism

```
Forcing yourself to learn the "wrong" way
              ↓
Brain makes associations that don't stick
              ↓
Existing knowledge starts going BLANK
              ↓
More forcing = more erasure
              ↓
System is SHOWING YOU it's the wrong technique
```

> "You are not using the right technique and therefore the whole thing is starting to go blank to illustrate to you that you're not using the technique that's correct for you. So it will erase more and more and more until you actually start using the technique that is actually more exciting for you to absorb what you need."

### The Proof — Passion-Driven Speaking

**Question**: "When you speak [to audiences], do you forget things?"

**Answer**: "No. My passion takes over and it just comes out."

> "When you come from that energy, you don't forget. You know what you need to know. That's the energy to come from in all of the ways in which you transmit that information."

### Learning Methods Compared

| Method | Effectiveness for This Person | Why |
|--------|------------------------------|-----|
| Writing modules with spouse, revising back and forth | Good start but not enough | Engages thinking but lacks full excitement |
| Listening to teachers repeatedly | Passive — not her style | One-directional absorption |
| **Speaking to live audiences** | **Perfect** — never forgets | **Passion takes over; different brain associations** |

### The Core Teaching on Learning

> "Stop doing it the way someone else would do it and start learning what you need to learn the way that you really would prefer to do it."

> "Use your imagination and find or create more imaginative, creative, and exciting ways to learn what it is that you actually need to learn."

### Why This Works

> "Because you're making different kinds of associations in your brain through having a different experience of learning those things — in a sense, they will stick. You'll know what you need to know because you'll have different associations to the information than the associations you're making now which don't work for the way in which you actually best absorb information based on the ways you're changing in life."

```
Wrong learning method for YOU
  → Wrong associations → Forgetting → Blanking out

Right learning method for YOU
  → Exciting associations → Knowing → Instant recall
```
