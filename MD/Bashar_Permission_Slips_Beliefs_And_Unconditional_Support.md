## Permission Slips - The Core Concept

### The Fundamental Teaching

> "All objects, all tools, all rituals, all techniques are permission slips you attract yourself to because they are representative of something within your belief system."

### How Permission Slips Work

```
Your Belief System
       ↓
Says: "If I use this tool/object/technique..."
       ↓
"...I can allow myself to be more of who I am"
       ↓
Permission granted to shift belief system
       ↓
New experiences become possible
```

### The Truth About Permission Slips

| What They Appear To Do | What They Actually Do |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| Have inherent power | Reflect your beliefs |
| Cause the change | Allow you to permit the change |
| Are necessary | Are temporarily useful |
| Work universally | Work because YOU believe they work |

### Key Insight

> "The things themselves don't really do anything. They are simply reflective of something within your belief system that says that now that you have this object, this tool, this technique, this ritual, now it's possible for you to shift your belief system."

### The Ultimate Permission Slip

> "Every single permission slip you attract in your life is simply for the ultimate purpose of reminding you that YOU are the ultimate permission slip. You're the one giving yourself permission to change and be more of who you are."

### The Evolution

1. **Stage 1**: You need external permission slips (objects, tools, rituals)
2. **Stage 2**: You recognize they're reflections of inner changes
3. **Stage 3**: You understand you are the one initiating the experience
4. **Stage 4**: You can access states directly without external aids

### Examples of Permission Slips

- Crystals
- Astrology
- Tarot card reading
- Any technique or ritual
- Teachers and teachings
- Even Bashar himself

> "This is what teachers do. This is what reflections are for. To remind you that you're the one that's initiating the experience anyway."

## Thinking vs Knowing - A Key Distinction

### The Three Levels

| Level | Characteristics | Power |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| **Thinking** | Mental process, uncertain | Weakest |
| **Believing** | Accepted as true, influences reality | Strong |
| **Knowing** | Synonymous with action, no doubt | Most Powerful |

### The Walking Example

> "If I asked you to walk across the room, you wouldn't stand there and go, 'Well, I believe I can. Let me see. Do I think I can walk across the room?' You would just walk. You wouldn't even have the dialogue because you KNOW that you can walk across the room."

### Knowing and Action Are Synonymous

```
When you KNOW something:
- No internal dialogue needed
- No questioning occurs
- Action happens automatically
- The state and the behavior are one
```

### The Critical Question

When asked "Do you believe you need to continue this experience to learn?"

**Answer revealing the gap**: "I don't THINK I do" or "I THINK I don't need it"

**The issue**: Thinking is not believing. Believing is not knowing.

> "You didn't say you BELIEVED or you KNEW that you didn't need it. Big difference."


## How Negative Beliefs Hide Themselves

### The Nature of Negative Beliefs

> "Negative beliefs will do everything in their power to maintain themselves, including hiding themselves as best as they can so that you don't know that they're there."

### Their Strategy

```
Negative Belief
       ↓
Creates fear of looking deeply
       ↓
Makes you think: "If I find it, it will be TRUE"
       ↓
You avoid looking
       ↓
Belief remains hidden and active
```

### The Fear It Creates

> "The negative belief makes you believe that if you dig down to find the negative belief, what you find out about yourself that's negative will actually be empirically true. And therefore you don't want to look because you're afraid that if you find it, that's what you actually will find out about yourself."

### The Guarantee

> "I guarantee you it never will be. That's just the smoke and mirrors from the negative belief doing everything in its power to prevent you from letting it go because that's what it's designed to do."

### Secondary Beliefs - The Decoy System

```
Core Negative Belief
       ↓
Surrounds itself with secondary beliefs
       ↓
You find a secondary belief
       ↓
Think: "Ah, I found it! Let it go!"
       ↓
Behavior doesn't change
       ↓
Core belief still operates
```

### How to Know You Found the Core Belief

> "If your behavior doesn't change after dropping it, then you haven't found the core belief. You've only found a secondary belief."

### The Mirror Never Lies

> "Physical reality is a mirror and will always reflect back to me what I truly believe. So if I'm having a negative experience in some way, that means you're still lurking around."

### Addressing the Belief

> "I'm going to find you and I'm going to tell you that you can relax, that your job is done. Well done. You played a good game, but now you can relax."

## Tracing Thoughts Back to Beliefs

### The Chain of Causation

```
BELIEF (Foundation)
    ↓
DEFINITION (How you define things)
    ↓
THOUGHT (What you think about it)
    ↓
FEELING (How you feel about it)
    ↓
BEHAVIOR (How you act)
```

### The Key Insight

> "If you don't have a definition of something, you don't know what to think about it. You don't know how to feel about it."

### The Example

> "If I give you a word that you have no definition for and ask you how do you feel about it, you wouldn't have a clue because you have no definition. But once you have the definition, you go, 'Ah, now I know how to feel about that. Now I know what to think about that.'"

### How to Use This

When you notice a negative thought:
1. Recognize the thought
2. Trace it back: "What belief must I have to generate this thought?"
3. Identify the underlying belief
4. Examine if it's the core belief or a secondary belief
5. When you find a truly negative belief out of alignment with your true self:

> "As soon as you identify it, it will automatically appear to be nonsensical and illogical and you'll drop it."

### The Test

> "If your behavior doesn't change after dropping it, then you haven't found the core belief."
