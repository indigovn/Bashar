> "Telepathy is not actually transmission over a distance per se. It is not reading each other's minds or projecting or sending in that sense really. It is that when you are in the same vibratory state as another, you have exactly the same thought at exactly the same moment."

But I do put myself into an altered state that vibrationally represents the idea that this dialogue exists within the greater idea of myself as an expression.
