When you doubt your worth, you are attempting to argue with existence about your existence — an argument you can never win.

## Why Sci-Fi Time Travel Doesn't Work

### The Fundamental Problem

> "Time travel in the way that your science fiction stories portray it is not really the way it works. It is all about the idea of shifting to parallel realities that may be very very similar to your own."

### The Detailed Breakdown

Bashar walks through a specific example using a life timeline (Baby → Child → Teen → Adult → Senior):

**The Scenario:** An adult invents time travel and goes back to meet their child self.

> "You would think as most of your sci-fi stories propose to you that you're actually going back to your childhood. You're not. In the sense that you recognize yourself as being an adult traveling back in time to meet a younger version of yourself — that's very different than what you experienced as a child not having met an older version of yourself."

### Why You Can't Go Back to Your Own Timeline

> "Those frames are fixed. They are those frames. The frame that just has you in it as a child and not your future self. So when you do then have the experience of traveling back in time and actually running into your younger self and introducing yourself as the future self, that's taking place in a parallel reality where in that reality that child met you."

```

                                       ↓
                              But Child₁ NEVER met
                              a future self. Those
                              frames are FIXED.
                                       ↓
                              You actually shift to...

PARALLEL TIMELINE (Timeline 2):
  [Baby₂] → [Child₂ + You] → [Teen₂] → [Adult₂] → [Senior₂]
                 ↑
         This child DID meet
         a "future self" — but
         from another timeline.
         This is Child₂, NOT Child₁.

WHEN YOU "RETURN" TO YOUR TIME:
  Timeline 1: [Adult₁] = BLANK (you left, frame is empty forever)
  You arrive in Timeline 3: [Senior₃] (different history now)
```

### The Key Insight

> "That frame doesn't include you anymore. It's blank and it always will be. You can't go back to that moment. You can't go back to that reality."

> "You're skipping all over the place becoming another version, in a sense shifting your consciousness to become another version in another parallel reality of yourself."
