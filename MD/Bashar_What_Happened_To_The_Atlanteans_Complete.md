## Q&A: Fear of the Unknown and the Physical Mind

### The Situation

Someone passionate about communicating with multi-dimensional beings experiences fear when sensing presences, shutting down the experience.

### The Deep Exploration

**Q: Why do you get afraid?**
A: "I'm afraid that I'm going to be taken advantage of."

**Q: Why would you attract something that would take advantage of you?**
A: "Because I'm not in tune enough."

**Q: Why do you think that about yourself? What are you measuring yourself against?**
A: "This amazing ideal that I'm supposed to be."

### The Core Issue

> "You have a definition that is making you feel that without the conscious mind knowing every single little detail that something's going to go wrong."

### The Physical Mind vs Higher Mind

```
PHYSICAL MIND                    HIGHER MIND
- Not designed to be in control  - Designed to guide
- Experience fully what comes    - Knows what needs to happen
- Like a diving mask             - Like the diver
- Sees clearly what's here       - Knows what's coming
- Works WITH higher mind         - Steers the journey
```

### The Diving Mask Analogy

> "If you go deep sea diving and you use a diving mask, which is only designed to allow you to see clearly what's happening under the water instead of it being murky. It's like saying the diving mask has to know everything you're about to see before you're willing to see it. The diving mask isn't designed to tell you what's coming up or what fish you're going to see around the next bend. It's just designed to let you see whatever comes up more clearly. That's its job."

### The Solution

> "Stop putting so much pressure on your physical mind to do a job it's not designed to do. And let the higher mind take over because that's its job."

### The Physics of Positive States

> "You cannot experience anything but a positive outcome if you stay in a positive state because that's just physics. What you put out is what you get back."

### Handling Uncomfortable Moments

> "Even if you momentarily go into a place that feels uncomfortable, know that that's just there to get your attention that you're holding on to something that doesn't work for you."

### The Practice

1. If you momentarily shut down an experience, go back and visit it later
2. Explore it from a positive point of view
3. Know that anything has the ability to serve you positively
4. Relax and open up
5. Let it be whatever it is
6. Know it can only give you a positive outcome if you stay positive
