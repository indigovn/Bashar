## The Polarity Object — A Lucid Dreaming Permission Slip

### About Permission Slips

> "Permission slips are simply those objects, those tools, those rituals, those ideas, those techniques that simply allow you based on your belief system to grant yourself more permission to be more of who you are."

Some permission slips work for the collective consciousness; some are designed for the individual. Your imagination may tweak this technique — that's fine.

### The Complete 28-Day Technique

#### Week 1 (Days 1-7): Create Your Joy Object

**Create or acquire an object** that is a symbol of your highest joy, your essence, your passion.

| It Can Be | Examples |
|-----------|---------|
| Something you draw | A painting, sketch, mandala |
| Something you sculpt | Clay figure, carved wood |
| Something you buy | Art piece, glass object, meaningful item |
| Something you find | Natural object, crystal, stone |
| Something you build | Woodwork, metalwork |
| Simple or complex | A colored ball, a piece of clothing, anything |

**Requirements**:
- You must be **strongly attracted** to it
- It must represent **your essence, joy, passion**
- It should be **small enough to hold** in your hand
- When you touch it, look at it, you **feel** it represents who you truly are

**Daily Practice — Week 1**:
- Meditate with the object **15 minutes**, at least **once per day** (twice ideal — morning and evening before sleep)
- Look at it, feel it, turn it around, discover everything about it
- Refine it if needed to be more representative of you
- Get deeply, intimately **familiar** with it

#### Week 2 (Days 8-14): Add the Mirror

**Attach a small mirror or shard of mirror** to the object.

```
┌──────────────────┐
│                  │
│   Joy Object     │
│                  │
│   ┌────────┐     │

│   │  eyes)  │    │

│                  │
└──────────────────┘
```

**Daily Practice — Week 2**:
- Same 15-minute meditation, once or twice daily
- Handle the object AND **look at your reflection** in the mirror
- **Look into your own eyes** — make sure you see yourself looking back at you
- Continue getting familiar with the whole assembly

#### Week 3 (Days 15-21): Add the Note

**Attach a small note** next to the mirror that says: **"You are dreaming."**

- Any form of writing, any color you wish
- Place it **right next to the mirror** so you see both simultaneously

**Daily Practice — Week 3**:
- Same meditation
- Look at the object, look in the mirror, **read the note**
- See the note and mirror simultaneously
- Continue building deep familiarity

#### Week 4 (Days 22-28): Add the Opposite Object — The Polarity Shock

This is the **critical** step. Attach an object that is the **exact opposite** of your joy energy — something jarring, incompatible, startling.

**Examples of opposite objects**:
- A nail on a soccer ball
- A razor blade on something soft
- Anything that is **vibrationally incompatible** with you
- Something you would **never** imagine belonging on your joy object
- Something that makes it almost **dangerous to handle**

### Why the Opposite Object Works

```
Weeks 1-3: Object becomes so familiar
  → It starts appearing in your DREAMS
              ↓
In the dream, you see the mirror
  → Might remind you you're dreaming (but might not)
              ↓

  → Might trigger lucidity (but might not)
              ↓
In the dream, you see the OPPOSITE OBJECT
  → SHOCK — "I would NEVER put this on my object!"
  → "This is so wrong, so jarring, so out of context..."
  → "I MUST be dreaming!"
              ↓
LUCIDITY ACHIEVED
  → You remember you put it there for exactly this purpose
  → You are now AWAKE in the dream
```

> "It will be so startling to see it there, so jarring, so opposite of what you would prefer to be on that object that that shock will remind you — oh, I'm dreaming. I have to be dreaming because I would never prefer this to be on this object."
