The heart is revealed as the primary receiver of higher mind communication, translating it into the sensation of passion, and as the organ that connects all beings through expanding electromagnetic bubbles — a process Bashar calls "telempathy."

## The Physical Heart and Electromagnetic Field

### The Physical Mechanism

> "Your heart, as you know, beats to pump your blood through your veins and arteries around your body, circulating oxygen to your systems so they may absorb it and thrive and function properly."

But the heart does far more than circulate oxygen. The blood contains **iron**, and as it moves through the body, this iron-rich blood generates and amplifies the body's **electromagnetic field**.

### How It Works

| Component | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| **Heart** | Pumps blood; generates strongest electromagnetic pulse |
| **Blood** | Contains iron; carries electromagnetic properties |
| **Circulation** | Movement of iron-rich blood generates the EM field |
| **Heartbeat** | Each beat sends an electromagnetic bubble expanding from the body |

### Refining the Field

> "As you allow yourself more clarity, more refinement in your physical form, as you remove toxins from your system that may interfere with this process of your blood circulation, this electromagnetic field becomes more and more refined and extends farther and farther from your body."

| Body State | EM Field Quality |
|------------|-----------------|
| Toxin-laden, unclear | Weak, limited range |
| Cleansed, refined | Strong, extended range |
| Ascending, high frequency | Highly sensitive, far-reaching |

## Telempathy: The Emotional Intelligence of the Heart

### Why "Telempathy" Instead of "Telepathy"

> "Which again is why we say telempathy instead of telepathy, because the empathy underscores the emotional component of the heart. The emotional intelligence of the heart that communicates with every beat to every other heart on the planet."

| Term | Components | Emphasis |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Telepathy** | Tele + pathy | Distance + feeling (general) |
| **Telempathy** | Tele + empathy | Distance + **emotional resonance** |

### The Key Distinction

Bashar deliberately uses "telempathy" to emphasize that:
- Heart-to-heart communication is fundamentally **emotional**
- The heart has its own **emotional intelligence**
- Every heartbeat is an act of **empathic communication**
- This communication happens with **every beat** to **every other heart on the planet**

> "You are living expanding bubbles of electromagnetic energy that expand and issue from the heart with every single beat in constant connection and communication with one another."
