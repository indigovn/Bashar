## Key Principles Summary

### On Love and Existence
- Unconditional love is the vibratory frequency of existence itself
- Your emotional feeling of love is your physical translation of that frequency
- Different dimensions represent different frequencies/reflections of love
- Pure synchronicity is when love manifests in everything you do

### On Permission Slips
- ALL tools, techniques, rituals, and objects are permission slips
- You are the one doing the work — the tool just gives you permission
- Water rituals, Reiki, chakras, crystals — all permission slips
- The goal is to reach pure synchronicity without needing the slips

### On Consciousness and Body
- Your body is a projection of your consciousness, not the reverse
- You are creating physical experience through a type of "magic"
- Words are spells — "spelling" literally means casting vibrations
- Physical reality is something you have conjured

### On Belief Systems
- Release negative beliefs that contradict your true self
- Release positive beliefs that no longer serve who you're becoming
- The process of refinement is infinite — it never ends
- Beliefs must be relevant for the arena you're playing in

### On Passion and Action
- Passion is moment-by-moment, not a lifespanning career
- Pick the option that's even slightly more exciting than any other
- Act with no insistence on what the outcome should be
- Passion CAN and WILL support you — the only block is believing it can't
- The journey matters as much as the destination

### On Goals and Visualization
- Goals are useful to get you into an excited state of being
- Once excited — completely and utterly drop the goal
- Your higher mind knows the best outcome (you don't)
- The result may look nothing like what you visualized — and be better

### On Wanting vs. Needing
- Wanting is its own state — it's the vibration of NOT having
- You always get what you need
- Wanting is often the negative ego thinking it needs something to be happy
- Ask: why would I want anything other than what I truly need?

### On Right, Wrong, and Power
- Right and wrong = alignment vs. resistance to existence
- People who harm others are disconnected from their inner power
- External control is an expression of believing you are disempowered
- Teaching people their true power eliminates "wrong" choices

### On the Three Minds
- Higher mind sends guidance as vibration
- Heart reflects that vibration like a drum
- Head mind filters through belief systems and feeds back to heart
- Alignment = steady heartbeat; misalignment = heart skips a beat

### On Others' Paths
- Love unconditionally — allow others to choose their path
- Be the example, not the enforcer
- Everyone is eternal — there's no rush
- "I love you no matter what you believe"

### On Symbols and Magic
- Dragons = dimensional beings translated into your understanding
- Unicorns = pinpoint focus on your true self, galloping unbridled
- Harry Potter = a more realistic representation of how reality works
- You are casting spells with your words every day

### On Perspective
- Where you stand determines your experience
- Sometimes you attract what you don't prefer to clarify what you do prefer
- The lesson can be as simple as: choose where you stand
