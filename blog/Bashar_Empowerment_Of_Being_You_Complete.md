# Bashar: The Empowerment of Being You - Complete Teaching

## Overview

This transmission, titled "The Empowerment of Being You," is a masterclass on self-empowerment delivered as a cohesive opening monologue. It addresses the paradox that being yourself should be the simplest thing — yet most people are carrying beliefs that aren't theirs. It reveals that those foreign beliefs serve a purpose (allowing you to discover who you are by finding out what you're NOT), explains the telltale sign of carrying beliefs that aren't yours (they weigh you down while what's truly yours weighs nothing and energizes you), redefines surrender as accepting the control you already have rather than giving up control, distinguishes between what you want and what you truly need, establishes that these teachings are not philosophy but an actual instruction manual for how reality works, introduces the concept of perfect timing and why you will never miss an appointment unless you worry about missing it, and emphasizes that excitement doesn't always mean jumping up and down — it can be calm, peaceful, meditative. The core message: your fundamental purpose is to be the unique you that you are as fully as you possibly can.

## The Paradox — Being Yourself Should Be Simple

### The Setup

> "Many people on your planet would think that it is the simplest thing to be who you are."

### The Reality

> "And yet, many of you are brought up in your societies buying into many different kinds of belief systems that have more to do with other people's beliefs than your own, than the ones that are necessarily fully aligned with who you are."

This is the central paradox of human experience: being yourself should require no effort at all — it's who you ARE. Yet most people are operating under layers of beliefs they adopted from family, society, and culture that have nothing to do with their true nature.

## How to Know You're Carrying Beliefs That Aren't Yours — They Weigh You Down

### The Test

> "How you know you are carrying around beliefs that aren't yours is that they weigh you down."

> "You feel resistance in your life to acting on your highest passion. There are always reasons that come up to not do so. There are always excuses that seem logical at the time. Those are your clues that you're carrying around ideas, beliefs that have nothing to do with you."

### What Belongs to You Feels Different

> "Those things that belong to you weigh nothing. They do not weigh you down. In fact, they energize you. They drive you forward."

> "Acting on your highest passion brings the driving engine into your life of energy and allows you to be very excited and very enthusiastic about getting up in the morning and doing whatever it is that excites you at that moment."

| Beliefs That ARE Yours | Beliefs That AREN'T Yours |
|----------------------|--------------------------|
| Weigh **nothing** | **Weigh you down** |
| **Energize** you | Create **resistance** |
| **Drive** you forward | Produce **excuses** that seem logical |
| Make you **excited** to get up | Make you **dread** the day |
| Feel like **freedom** | Feel like **heaviness** |
| Are your natural **engine** | Are artificial **brakes** |

### The Clue

> "When you begin to let go of those beliefs that are not yours, you will feel more empowerment. You will feel more freedom. You will feel lighter."

```
              ↓

  "Now I see more clearly what I DO prefer"
              ↓
  This creates CLARITY
              ↓
  Replace what isn't you
  with what IS you
              ↓
  Self-empowerment deepens
```

## Surrender Does Not Mean Giving Up Control

### The Common Misunderstanding

> "Understand that it's not about controlling things. It's about surrendering. And when we say surrendering, it's not about giving up control. If that's the way you're interpreting the concept of surrender, then you have a definition that doesn't work and is out of alignment with your true essential core."

### What Surrender Actually Means

> "Surrendering does not mean giving up control. Surrendering means allowing yourself to let go of what you think control should be and actually accept the control you already naturally have."

> "Because you all have it. You're all in control. You're just playing a game to pretend that you're not."

### Surrendering to the Current

> "It's about letting go and going with the flow of the current that is truly you because that current will carry you exactly where you need to be carried."

> "You're surrendering to the control you already have by letting yourself be carried by the current that is truly you to the places you really need to go."

| Giving up control | Accepting the control you already have |
| Becoming passive/helpless | Going with the flow of YOUR current |
| Letting others decide | Letting your TRUE self guide you |
| Weakness | The deepest form of trust |
| Losing power | Your current carries you where you need to go |

```
SURRENDER REDEFINED

  You THINK you need to CONTROL things
              ↓
  But you're ALREADY in control
  (you're just pretending you're not)
              ↓
  SURRENDER = let go of what you THINK
  control should be
              ↓
  ACCEPT the control you already
  naturally have
              ↓
  Go with the CURRENT that is truly you
              ↓
  That current carries you EXACTLY
  where you need to be carried
              ↓
  THAT is surrender:
  Surrendering TO your own power,
  not surrendering your power.
```
